Project Conclusion

This project gave our team the opportunity to deeply explore the human skeletal system, understand the challenges posed by skeletal diseases, and propose an innovative nano robotic solution that could one day contribute to medical science. Through teamwork, research, and hands-on programming, we developed skills in critical thinking, engineering design, and collaborification. Below are the individual conclusions from each team member.
