Welcome to Our Body System Website

Welcome to our interactive educational website dedicated to exploring one of the most fundamental and fascinating structures of the human body: the skeletal system. This site was created as our final PIA (Producto Integrador de Aprendizaje) project for the IAR 2026 course at CIDEB Mederos, Universidad Autónoma de Nuevo León.

The human skeleton is an extraordinary biological framework that includes 206 bones in the adult human body. It is the structural support that enables us to stand, move, and interact with our surroundings.

Our bones not only support us, but they also shield our important organs such as the brain, heart, and lungs. In the bone marrow, they generate blood cells and store essential minerals such as calcium and phosphorus.

In this website, you can find extensive research on the anatomy and physiology of the skeletal system, a deep analysis of the most common skeletal diseases and health problems that affect people around the world, and an innovative nano-robotic solution developed by our team to fight one of those critical health challenges.

Our team has worked collaboratively using multiple stages from initial research to the design of a 3D nano robot, the development of algorithms and flowcharts, and the construction of a physical robot prototype. This site consolidates all that work in one place where we share our journey, our conclusions and our common vision of using technology and robotics to better human health.

We invite you to explore each section using the navigation menu above. Thank you for visiting!
